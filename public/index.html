Mission Statement

At Shanti Ashram, our mission is to empower children who have been deprived of basic needs of life, by offering them a nurturing environment that encompasses shelter, nourishment, and education. We are dedicated to fostering an environment where these children not only flourish, but also harness their creative potential by finding their rightful role in our global community. As a compassionate sanctuary for children, we are fueled by a profound passion to make a lasting positive impact on their lives.
