Our Story

We are a diverse group of dedicated individuals committed to building an ashram/orphanage in Nepal, South Asia. The team will be focusing on fundraising, collaborating with businesses, and spreading awareness until November 2024. We are committed to do our best to develop our vision of empowering children through shelter, nourishment, and education. Your invaluable support and generous contributions in its entirety will be used to enhance the overall wellbeing of the children of the Shanti Ashram in Nepal.

Core Values

Structure

Childhood is a vulnerable time for children as they absorb their environment in detail. As human beings thrive in structured livestyles, it is important to build a stable foundation that aids the overall development of the child. Our Ashram will create a family that helps children turn their desires into reality and construct structure in their lives that lead to better outcomes.
