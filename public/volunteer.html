Explore our service opportunities

Our mission at Shanti Ashram is driven by a deep commitment to empower underprivileged children through shelter, nourishment, and education, allowing them to thrive and discover their creative potential. In line with our mission, you can find numerous opportunities to make a meaningful impact.

You can join us in providing educational support, mentoring, and tutoring sessions to help these children excel academically. Additionally, there are opportunities to engage in recreational activities, arts and crafts, and sports, fostering their creative development. If you have expertise in fundraising and community outreach, you can play a vital role in securing resources and expanding our reach to make an even greater difference in the lives of these children.

By volunteering at Shanti Ashram, you can become an integral part of our mission to create a brighter future for these deserving children, helping them find their rightful place in our global community.
